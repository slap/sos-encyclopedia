The Motzkin polynomial is
$$
M(x,y) = x^4 y^2 + x^2 y^4 - 3x^2 y^2 + 1.
$$

One SOS-like decomposition (identity to check) is:
$$
M(x,y) = (x^2y - 1)^2 + (xy^2 - 1)^2 + \text{(adjustment)}.
$$
